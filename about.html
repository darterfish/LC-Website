Est. 2014 · Ann Arbor, MI

The Only Business Development Firm Exclusively Focused on Expert Witnesses

Litigation Connection was founded with a single conviction: forensic accountants and valuation experts are extraordinarily good at what they do, but business development is a discipline of its own. Most experts grow their practices through referrals and reputation alone — which is slow and unpredictable.

We built a system that changes that. By combining a deep attorney intelligence database with proven outreach methodology, we put expert witnesses in front of attorneys who have an active, demonstrated need for exactly their expertise.

Over 5,000 appointments booked. More than a decade of attorney relationships built. A proprietary platform covering 180,000+ attorney profiles across all 50 states. And a done-for-you service that lets you focus on your cases while we grow your pipeline.

Our Approach
