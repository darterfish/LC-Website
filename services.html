How We Book Meetings

Precision Targeting. Personal Outreach.

We don't cold blast a list. Every outreach is tailored to the specific attorney's practice and matched against your background — the way a skilled business developer would do it.

The Matching Logic

We start by mapping your expertise — your specialty areas, case types you've worked, and the jurisdictions you serve. That profile becomes the filter for every attorney we target.

Our platform then scores attorneys across 345 practice subcategories, ranking those whose practice areas and case history most closely align with your background. The result is a highly qualified target list — not a generic directory.

Geography matters too. We geofence your outreach to your state and city preferences, so every call is reaching an attorney in a market you can realistically serve.

The Outreach

Our callers use AI-generated scripts built around each attorney's specific cases and practice. A script might reference a restructuring matter, a specific valuation dispute, or a known bankruptcy case — connecting the attorney's work directly to your expertise.

CVs and testimony samples are sent only when requested by the attorney. We never spray collateral — it keeps the conversation valuable and the relationship professional from day one.

Once a meeting is booked, you receive a calendar invite immediately. We send the attorney a day-of reminder to keep show rates high.

Our Process

From Onboarding to First Meeting

1

Data Review & Scoping

We review your background, testimony history, and existing attorney relationships. We establish your geographic geofence, practice focus, and monthly meeting quota.

2

Expert Profile Setup

We configure your expert profile in our platform — focus specialty, keyword tiers, geographic targets, and contact information for outreach signatures.

3

Attorney Matching & Scoring

Our platform searches and scores the attorney universe against your profile. We build your target list — typically attorneys who align at the highest tier of keyword relevance.

4

Outreach Begins

Our callers begin working the list using personalized scripts. CV and testimony are emailed only on attorney request — 10 to 20 per month on average.

5

Meeting Booked — You're Notified

A calendar invite lands in your inbox. You have access to our shared calendar 24/7. Day-of reminder is sent to the attorney. You show up and build the relationship.
