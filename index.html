How It Works

From Expert to Attorney in Three Steps

Whether you use our platform or our done-for-you service, the path to new attorney relationships is straightforward.

1

We Learn Your Focus

We map your expertise, testimony history, and geographic targets to define exactly which attorneys are the right fit — by practice area, jurisdiction, and seniority.

2

We Identify & Score

Our platform searches 180,000+ attorney profiles across 345 practice subcategories, scoring and ranking matches using AI analysis of bios, cases, and firm data.

3

Meetings Get Booked

We generate personalized cold call scripts and emails — or our team makes the calls for you. You receive calendar invites for Zoom or in-person meetings, ready to go.
