Attorney Search & Filtering

Find Exactly the Right Attorneys

Our database of 136,000+ attorneys is fully searchable across every dimension that matters. Narrow your universe before running AI matching to surface the highest-value targets for your outreach.

  • Search by name, firm, email domain, biography, or case keywords
  • Filter by state, city, practice area, attorney type, and firm size
  • Filter for attorneys who have cases or confirmed email addresses
  • 12,000+ firms searchable by name
  • Export results to CSV or push directly to your CRM
AI Match Scoring

Ranked Results Matched to Your Expertise

Our scoring engine analyzes attorney biography text, practice areas, case history, and firm data against your expert profile — producing a precision match score so you focus on the best opportunities first.

  • Multi-factor scoring across 345 practice subcategories
  • Three-tier keyword hierarchy for precise alignment
  • Concentration analysis — attorneys who specialize in your area rank higher
  • Geographic bonus scoring for your target jurisdictions
  • Batch workflow scores your entire filtered universe at once
